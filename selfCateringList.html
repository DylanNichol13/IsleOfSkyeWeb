Self Catering

Eyre Point Cottage

Eyre Point Cottage occupies a glorious position on the south west coast of the Island of Raasay, about 1 mile south of the ferry terminal at Suishnish. Standing quite alone at the water's edge and reached by a well-tended drive with a sheep gate, it looks out across the Sound with breathtaking views of the Cuillins on Skye.

Aurora Cottage

Aurora Cottage is a luxury home from home affording stunning sea views to the Outer Hebrides, breathtaking sunsets and dark skies perfect for stargazing, a photographer's paradise! Situated to the north of the spectacular Trotternish Peninsula, it is an ideal base for exploring Skye. The cottage sleeps upto 6 people, is fully equipped and perfect for those more relaxing days in. Book now and enjoy the tranquillity of Aurora in a beautiful location.

Cuan Beag

Peaceful shoreline location ten minutes from Portree. Sleeps up to six in a high level of comfort and extensive facilities, three bedrooms (two en-suite) and luxury bathroom.
Stunning views over Loch through large windows; underfloor heating throughout and wood burning stove for extra ambience. Relax and enjoy the ever-changing views and wildlife, use the direct access to shore for kayaks/small boats, or retreat to comfort after easily reaching most of Skye's attractions on days out.

Redhills Caravan

Redhills Caravan a large 3 bedroom static caravan which is situated between the Red and Black Cuillins in the picturesque village of Torrin near Broadford. Guests will be spoilt for choice with the uninterrupted views surrounding them - the majestic Bla Bheinn, Loch Slapin towards the Isle of Eigg - which can be seen through the caravan windows or from the large decking area outside. Pets welcome.

Sea Drift

This modern apartment is adjacent to The Skye Picture House guesthouse but is entirely self-contained and has its own entrance.
Open plan kitchen and sitting room with glorious views. One ground floor single bedroom and bathroom with disabled access. Two upstairs bedrooms, one ensuite. Sleeps up to 5. Very well equipped and comfortably furnished. Rental includes heating, hot water, electricity and hire of bed-linen and towels.

House on the Brae

This lovely new cottage stands on the hillside in one of Skye's most secluded glens. Nestled beneath the mountains Ben Aslak and Sgurr na Coinnich, Kylerhea is a wildlife paradise and this cottage is perfectly positioned with stunning panoramic views of the glen. Otters, seals, deer and eagles are amongst the abundant wildlife to be seen daily. Modern facilities. Entrance porch; spacious lounge, fully equipped kitchen/dining room; modern bathroom and sleeps 4 comfortably in two bedrooms.

Inver Rose

Inver Rose is a spacious detached bungalow in a peaceful location on the outskirts of Kyleakin village. 1/3 acre garden. Comfortably sleeps six, but also popular with couples. Spacious lounge, with new Panasonic 50" plasma TV and Blu ray player. The cottage has panoramic views over Kyleakin, and the 14th Century Castle Moil to the Sea and the Kintail mountains. Inver Rose is the perfect base for Skye, Kintail and Wester Ross.
