Bed & Breakfast

The Old Post Office

Renovated croft house situated on the Trotternish peninsula. We have visitscotland 3 star rating and also offer evening meals. Our B&B has the "Taste of Scotland" award for our locally sourced ingredients, and are all homemade. We are ideally placed for not only the ferry to the outer islands, but also for exploring the many sights that Skye has to offer. The Quiraing and Old Man of Storr are all within easy reach.

RockView

With sea views out over Portree Bay, Rockview Bed & Breakfast makes a beautiful place to stay. Conveniently located, just a 2 minute walk from Portree Square. With two large comfortable double bedrooms both with en-suite shower rooms. We serve a full Scottish cooked breakfast or a continental style breakfast. Stay at RockView on your next visit to Skye.

ViewMount

Our Bed and Breakfast is a 5 minute walk from Portree Town Centre. We have 2 family rooms and 3 double rooms all ensuite. We also have a large private car park for our visitors use and also a private lounge. Next door to Viewmount we have our Cottage with 2 double rooms and 1 family room all en suite, next door again we have Woodside Bed and Breakfast with 3 family rooms ensuite.

Dunborrodale

Dunborrodale bed and breakfast is owned and run by Calum and Rachel Simmister. The bed and breakfast has 3 bedrooms, each room has tea and coffee making facilities as well as small fridges and tvs. Toiletries are also provided. Breakfast is served in a spacious dining room with a selection of continental options as well as a fully cooked Scottish breakfast.

Feochan Guest House

Feochan Guest House is easy to find. As you come into Portree from the south you will see the green "Welcome to PORTREE" signs on each side of the road. The NEXT opening on the right leads to two houses. The left one (signposted) is Feochan. Off road parking is right outside the cottage.
A warm welcome awaits you.

Cuil Lodge

Enjoy every comfort in this guest house designed to give you that "home from home" feeling. All rooms have sea views over the bay, and are ensuite. All rooms have tea and coffee making facilities, free Wi-Fi, and flat screen TV carrying a selection of digital TV and radio channels.
