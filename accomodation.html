Accomodation

Bed & Breakfast

A room for the night and Breakfast provided in the morning. Allowing you to explore the Island during the day, sample one of the many Restaurants in the evening and then back to a clean bed for the night.

Hotel

The Isle of Skye has a great range of Hotels to suit many budgets, from a basic 2 star to 5 star Luxury. Most Hotels offer a range of meals and comfortable room to impress. Stay in a Hotel to enjoy a spot of the Highlife, but get out during the day to explore our rugged Island

Hostel

Hostels are considered the budget option when visiting the Isle of Skye. Perfect for the more adventurous that are happy with shared facilities and sometimes shared rooms.

Camping

Often considered the 'budget' option, but camping on Skye when surrounded by incredible scenery is not such a bad option. It could even be considered the romantic option!

Self Catering

Self Catering is a great option for those who want to have flexibility during their stay. No meals are provided, but facilities are available for you to cook if you wish. Eat in or eat out, come and go as you please, you have control of when and what you do. Allows you to stick to a budget if that is a concern.
