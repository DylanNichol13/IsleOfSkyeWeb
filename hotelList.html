Hotels

Sligachan Hotel

Sligachan is the most centrally located hotel on the Isle of Skye, making it the ideal base for exploring. We sit at the foot of the Cuillins the most stunning mountain range in Scotland.

Taigh Ailean Hotel

Our dog & family-friendly hotel is situated at the end of the beautiful Minginish Peninsular with spectacular views of Loch Harport.
We are a small hotel close to the Cuillin Mountains, the Talisker Distillery and the Fairy Pools making an ideal base for walkers, climbers, photographers, fishing or just pottering around experiencing the varied beauty of the island.

Taigh Ailean Hotel

The beautifully refurbished Broadford Hotel was the original home of Drambuie, where the liquor was first made. The Hotel overlooks Broadford Bay and has an excellent reputation for first class food created using fresh local produce. Rooms are chic and designed using Drambuie tartan.

Isle of Raasay Distillery Hotel

Treat yourself to an unforgettable experience and stay in our luxury accommodation at the first legal distillery on the Isle of Raasay. Enjoy amazing views over to The Isle of Skye. All guests have access to the Executive Lounge which is fully stocked with refreshments and snacks.
