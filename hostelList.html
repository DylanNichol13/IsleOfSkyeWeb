The Cowshed

The Cowshed Boutique Bunkhouse provides a luxurious but affordable retreat in the North End of Skye. Conveniently located near the Uig Ferry Terminal, there are stunning views across the bay from the living space, as well as a wood burning stove which makes for a cosy environment. Saucy Mary's Lodge

Saucy Mary's Lodge offers budget Skye accommodation in the village of Kyleakin right by the Skye Bride.
We have a range of room options form shared hostel rooms ideal for those on tight budget to en-suite double rooms. We also have a popular restaurant serving fresh local produce and a bar with regular live music.

The Flora Macdonald Hostel

The hostel has stunning views over the Sound of Sleat to the mountains of Scottish mainland.
Situated only 2.5 miles from the Armadale ferry on a traditional Skye croft. This is ideal budget accommodation. The Hostel have five private guest rooms (3 en-suite) as well as two kitchens for guest self catering. All rooms have bunk-beds.

Dun Flodigarry

Dun Flodigarry is a family run hostel in the beautiful crofting and Gaelic speaking township of Flodigarry on the Trotternish peninsula of Skye.
Our aim is to provide affordable accommodation for all. We have family, dorm, double en-suite and twin rooms available as well as some camping space. The Hostel is in the perfect location for walking in the famous Quiraing and has amazing views of Flodigarry island and the Torridon Mountains.
